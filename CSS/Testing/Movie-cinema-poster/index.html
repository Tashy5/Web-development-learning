Demon Slayer: Infinity Castle

Kimetsu no Yaiba The Movie

Overview

As the final battle against Muzan Kibutsuji unfolds, Tanjiro Kamado and the Demon Slayer Corps are drawn into the mysterious and ever-shifting Infinity Castle. This labyrinth of twisting halls and deadly beauty becomes the stage for humanity’s last stand against the Demon King.

Bound by courage, sacrifice, and unbreakable resolve, Tanjiro, Nezuko, Zenitsu, and Inosuke fight alongside the Hashira in an explosive confrontation that will decide the fate of both demons and humans. Stunning animation, heart-stopping battles, and emotional storytelling collide in this breathtaking climax to the Demon Slayer saga. Experience the ultimate showdown. Witness the dawn after endless night.
